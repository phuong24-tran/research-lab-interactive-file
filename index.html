B.Com Program Renewal

Reimagining the First Year of the B.Com at Telfer

Recommendations from the “Pedagogical Prefiguration” workshops, co-created by professors, students, and educational advisors to transform the first-year learning experience.

Lead: Mathieu Bouchard (Coordinator)
Report: Hamza Tawdi (Research Assistant)
From co-creation to program-wide recommendations

A cultural and pedagogical shift

This initiative goes beyond a simple content reform. It aims to rethink how we teach, learn, and collaborate in the first year of the B.Com. Drawing on three participatory workshops, the project identifies shared challenges and translates them into five strategic axes:

Coherent governance Support for teaching staff Living classroom experience Student-centred journey Responsible AI & integrity
Axis 1
Governance of Innovation
Dialogue & Coherence

A shared governance structure to align decisions, ensure transparency, and connect student and professor voices.

  • Create a permanent Student–Professor Innovation Committee.
  • Monitor the implementation and impact of pedagogical reforms.
  • Adopt a shared Pedagogical Quality Guide for all B.Com courses.
  • Harmonize course outlines and Brightspace standards across sections.
Axis 2
Supporting the Teaching Staff
Teaching Excellence

Recognizing pedagogy as a core dimension of academic excellence and supporting instructors in a changing learning environment.

  • Launch the Telfer Teaching Excellence certification program.
  • Focus on active learning, digital innovation, and inclusive pedagogy.
  • Build a Telfer Teaching Forum as a community of practice.
  • Encourage mentoring between experienced and new instructors.
Axis 3
Pedagogical Relationship
Engagement & Feedback

Turning the classroom into a collective learning space, supported by continuous feedback and mutual trust.

  • Shift away from purely transmissive lectures to interactive sessions.
  • Use short cycles: input, application, discussion.
  • Implement monthly micro-surveys in Brightspace for real-time feedback.
  • Encourage instructors to share how they act on student feedback.
Axis 4
Student Experience
Coherent Journey

Designing a first year that feels structured, meaningful, and supported—both in person and online.

  • Create a credit-bearing “Living and Learning at Telfer” integration course.
  • Clarify the progression of competencies across first-year courses.
  • Develop “Telfer Essentials,” a central digital hub integrated with Brightspace.
  • Empower students to co-govern and animate the digital environment.
Axis 5
Artificial Intelligence & Integrity
Ethical Innovation

Framing AI as a tool for critical thinking and learning, while cultivating a strong culture of academic integrity.

  • Adopt a faculty-wide policy on AI use with clear levels: allowed, guided, prohibited.
  • Require transparent disclosure when AI is used in assignments.
  • Introduce training on AI literacy and digital critical thinking.
  • Launch an “Augmented Integrity” Pact and workshops on ethics & AI.
